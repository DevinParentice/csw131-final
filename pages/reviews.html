The kaiju fights are a definite improvement over King of the Monsters, and so is the movie overall. The human elements of the story are the weakest as usual, but there is enough in the cinematography department to make up for it.

Raya and the Last Dragon (2021)

The movie takes many different elements from past Disney films, much to its fault. It causes the film to feel very familiar, as if you have already seen it before. As usual with Disney, the characters are funny and the story is touching.
